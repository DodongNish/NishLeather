About Us

Our Story

Just as every masterpiece begins with a crafter’s supplies, NishLeather’s world-class company was built from small beginnings. Founded as a shoe shop in 1973 by Ryuichi Nishimura, NishLeather would eventually grow to become an elite manufacturer of quality leather and nylon products and distributor of supplies for harnessmakers, saddlemakers and a wide range of other craftspeople.

While the original shoe shop’s primary focus was shoe repair, Ryuichi saw opportunities to expand the business into other markets. The company began manufacturing leather horse halters, which opened the door to sell to area harness and tack shops.

In 1983, the company began distributing leather and hardware under the Nish Distributing Co. name in response to a growing demand among its customers for raw materials, and the supply division was born. The following year NishLeather Goods and Nish Distributing Co. merged to form NishLeather, and the company’s first consignment auction was held. Known as a must-attend event, this annual auction is still bringing people from across the country together today to buy and sell a wide range of machinery, tools, and leather.

As the supply division grew, NishLeather produced its first 36-page supply catalog featuring leather, hardware, tools and supplies in 1990. The world-class company now offers one of the best product selections in the business. With leather, hardware, tools, leather care, equipment, sewing machines and more, NishLeather is the supplier of choice for both professionals and hobbyists. As a supplier with manufacturing experience, NishLeather knows that having the right supplies is essential to the success of any project.

NishLeather is pleased to announce the creation of a new division: NishLeathercraft. Crafters no longer need a wholesale account to purchase goods online. All items sold under NishLeather Supply are now available to end consumers at retail pricing through the convenience of our long-awaited NishLeathercraft website. The newly launched website features quality products, valuable tips, easy-to-follow project tutorials and more as a way to support the creative endeavors of our customers. Let NishLeathercraft’s superior products and unmatched customer service assist you with your next project.
