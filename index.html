Leatherworking course

Leather work and leather-craft courses, kits, live classes, gifts and online workshops from Japanese makers. Leather improves with age, which is why leather crafts are so satisfying! Handmade leather goods are not only stylish, but will have a durability you will enjoy for years to come. Leather craft involves cutting, sewing (often hand stitching), dyeing, lining, punching holes and generally fashioning your piece of hide into bags, belts, shoes, saddles, waistcoats, chaps, book covers and anything else you want to learn how to make. Experienced leather workers are a rare find these days; leather work tutors even more so. Browse leather courses, kits & gifts and discover the skills of leather bag, shoe and belt making, upholstery, leather dyeing, carving, embossing and stamping.
